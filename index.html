    Our Story & Mission

    Bridging the Digital Divide in Maiduguri

    Founded in the heart of Borno State, Salma Technologies LTD emerged from a simple vision: to provide accessible, professional digital solutions that empower businesses, students, and organizations in Northeast Nigeria.

    We started as a small tech support service and have grown into a comprehensive hub offering technology solutions, creative design, professional printing, and ICT training. Our journey reflects our commitment to the region's growth and digital transformation.

    • Integrated Approach: One-stop solutions for all digital needs
    • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of Maiduguri's business landscape
    • Youth Empowerment: Training the next generation through ST Academy
    • Quality & Integrity: Every project meets our high standards of excellence

    General Questions

    Where is Salma Technologies located?

    Our main office is located in Maiduguri, Borno State, Nigeria. We serve clients across Northeast Nigeria and can work remotely with clients anywhere in the country.

    Address: Shop No. 4 Zannari Venture's Hadiza Memorial Street Second Transformer, Maiduguri Borno State Nigeria.

    What are your working hours?

    Our office is open:

    • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
    • Saturday: Closed (Emergency IT support available)
    • Sunday: Closed (Emergency IT support available)

    For urgent matters outside these hours, you can reach us via WhatsApp or email.

    Web & App Development

    How long does it take to build a website?

    Development time varies based on complexity:

    • Basic Website (5 pages): 2-3 weeks
    • Business Website (10-15 pages): 4-6 weeks
    • E-commerce Store: 6-8 weeks
    • Custom Web Application: 8-12 weeks

    We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

    Do you provide website hosting?

    Yes! We offer reliable Nigerian-based hosting with:

    • 99.9% Uptime Guarantee
    • Free SSL Certificate
    • Daily Backups
    • 24/7 Technical Support
    • Email Accounts Setup

    Hosting is included in our web development packages for the first year.

    Design & Printing

    What file formats do you accept for printing?

    We accept all common file formats:

    • Preferred: PDF (print-ready), AI, PSD, CDR
    • Accepted: JPG, PNG, TIFF, DOC, PPT
    • Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI for quality prints

    If you're unsure about your files, bring them to our office and we'll help you prepare them for printing.

    SalmaTech Academy

    Do I need prior experience to join your courses?

    No! Our courses are designed for both beginners and those with some experience:

    • Beginner Courses: No prior knowledge needed
    • Intermediate Courses: Basic computer skills required
    • Advanced Courses: Previous experience recommended

    We assess each student's level during registration and recommend the appropriate starting point.

    Do you offer certificates after training?

    Yes! Upon successful completion of any course, you receive:

    • Official SalmaTech Academy Certificate
    • Digital Portfolio of your work
    • Letter of Recommendation (for outstanding students)
    • ITF/SIWES students receive Industrial Training Certificate

    Our certificates are recognized by local businesses and can boost your career prospects.

    What is the ITF/SIWES program?

    The Industrial Training Fund (ITF) and Students Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) are government programs that provide practical training for students. At SalmaTech:

    • Duration: 3-6 months (depending on school requirements)
    • Format: Hands-on training with real client projects
    • Areas: Web Development, Graphic Design, IT Support
    • Outcome: Practical skills + ITF/SIWES logbook signing
    Payment & Process

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept multiple payment options:

    • Bank Transfer: All Nigerian banks
    • Cash: At our office in Maiduguri
    • POS: Card payments at our office
    • Online Payment: Coming soon

    For large projects, we offer flexible payment plans (50% deposit, 50% on completion).

    Do you offer after-sales support?

    Absolutely! Our support includes:

    • Websites: 1-3 years free maintenance (depending on package)
    • Training: Lifetime email support for graduates
    • IT Support: 30-day warranty on repairs
    • Design/Print: Free minor revisions within 7 days

    We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients.

    What is your refund policy?

    Our refund policy varies by service:

    • Academy Courses: 80% refund if canceled 7 days before start
    • Web Development: Deposit refundable if project hasn't started
    • Printing Services: No refund on completed prints
    • IT Support: Full refund if issue isn't resolved

    All refund requests are reviewed case-by-case with customer satisfaction as our priority.
